Study with Monash

Wherever you're headed, you'll find a course at Monash that can get you there,
Our range of programs include:

Specialist Degrees

If you know what you want to do and are looking for a direct route into a particular field or career, then a specialist degree might be right for you.

Double Degrees

A Monash double degree lets you study towards two degrees at the same time. You can combine two undergraduate degrees and complete the degrees in two years less than if you did them separately. We also offer double degrees that incorporate an undergraduate degree and graduate master’s degree, accelerating your progress to a higher level of qualification.

Comprehensive Degrees

These courses offer a wide range of majors allowing you to build your studies around your professional and personal needs. You have the option to choose your major at the start of your course, or study a range of options before narrowing your focus.

Graduate Degrees

Our wide range of graduate degrees can help you advance your career, qualify to practice in a profession or allow you to expand your knowledge and skills into a new discipline.

International Experience

To take on the world you need a truly internationla outlook. Monash offers students the opportunitiy to become genuine global thinkers with campuses in Australia, Malaysia, China, India, and Italy.

Group of 8 University

Monash University is a founding member of the Group of 8, Australia's leading universities.

Size and Scale

A big university provides you with big opportunities. As Australia's largest university, with over 60,000 students and 300,000 alumni from over 170 countries, we are well placed to offer the benefits that brings. We know our students thrive in this inclusive and truly empowering environment.

Top 100 University World Ranking

Our world-class reputation for excellence in education and research coupled with our global record of innovation has seen Monash University ranked 73rd in the world, securing a place in the top 100 of world universities (Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2015-2016).

A Modern University

At just 50 years young, Monash has a contemporary world-view. We are continually driving change and improvement and have a proud history of preparing studetns for careers in the modern world.

Education Fused with Enterprise

Integrating work and industry experience comes naturally at Monash. You'll be encouraged to develop practical expertise alongside leadership and entrepreneurship skills, through a range of programs and initiatives at a personal level or associated with your area of study.

Staff and research
