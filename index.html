Flexible Learning

Specialist
Degree

If you know what you want to do and are looking for a direct route into a particular field or career, then a specialist degree might be right for you.

Comprehensive
Degree

These courses offer a wide range of majors allowing you to build your studies around your professional and personal needs. You have the option to choose your major at the start of your course, or study a range of options before narrowing your focus.

Double
Degree

A Monash Double Degree lets you pursue two areas of interest at the same time. You can qualify with two degrees in as little as 4 years, so you can combine all your interests while saving time and money.

International Experience

To take on the world you need a truly international outlook. Monash offers students the opportunity to become genuine global thinkers with campuses in Australia, Malaysia, China India and Italy. 

We also offer one of the most extensive international exchange and study tour programs with opportunities available in 30 countries around the globe.

Courses and Specifications

When it comes to study, we know that one size does not fit all so we have a wide range of courses for you to choose from. Whether you have a passion for the arts, are focused on a career in medicine, or want to pursue a career in the global business world, there's a course to suit you. You can choose an education experience that will prepare you for a successful career.
